who am i!

  • third year at harvard studying folklore and mythology and statistics with a minor in cs
    • why folk and myth? - in search of the absolute truth today whether that be in the form of disinformation campaigns or accurate and compelling journalism, i believe it is necessary to study the history of oral transmission and storytelling. also i just love studying children's literature and myths, the history of trolls, and food folklore.
    • why statistcs? - it is possible to use data and statistics to fit any narrative, and that is such a ominous thought. i believe knowledge of statistcal methods, theory, and models will help me communicate my ideas in a clear and truthful manner and call out statistically significant findings that do not subscribe to verifyably sound analysis.
    • why computer science? - coding is fun! and creative! and challenging! i love creating visual projects and documenting my process. i love sharing this skill with other people, especially women and young learners. learning and creating with computer science can open so many doors and i want to help people realize this!
