resume

i'm currently the food and features intern for the washington post. in a previous life, i was a data wrangler, elections nerd, and maps enthusiast at places like npr and the washington post. but more recently, i realized that writing, cooking, and dreaming about food makes my heart skip like no other job i've held. and unlike other jobs, food writing has stuck. i still code and tinker with digital art projects in my free time. if you're curious, you can find my food and data writing in the washington post, npr and atlas obscura.

i'm a third generation chinese american who calls san francisco home. my interests wax and wane but *currently* i love modern dance, anything by lorrie moore and fyodor dostoevsky, the broken social scene, the golden state warriors, ancient food, and discovering new cookbooks.
