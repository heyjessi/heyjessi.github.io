i'm a food, culture and interactive graphics reporter based in new york city. i've also spent a good chunk of my life in boston and san francisco. previously, i worked with elections data and maps at places like npr and the washington post. but it was there i learned that writing about and cooking food are my callings.

i'm a third generation chinese american who hails from san francisco. my interests are always in flux but i *currently* love modern dance, short stories and novels, the broken social scene, ancient food and discovering new cookbooks.
